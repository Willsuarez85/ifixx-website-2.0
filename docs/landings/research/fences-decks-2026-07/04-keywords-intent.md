## 4. Intent Classification Summary — BID vs AVOID

### BID AT LAUNCH (core budget)
**Fence:**
- **F1 Core Install** + **F4 Privacy/Wood** — highest commercial intent, best material fit for iFIXX. Primary fence ad group.
- **F2 Replacement** + **F3 Repair** (with size/qualifier ad copy) — strong repair-vs-replace angle, iFIXX's wheelhouse.
- **F8 Hyper-local** — cheap, high-intent, low competition. Run as tightly-matched local ad group.

**Deck:**
- **D1 Core Build** — top priority, highest value + volume. Primary deck ad group.
- **D2 Repair/Rebuild** — repair-vs-rebuild positioning, iFIXX sweet spot.
- **D5 Porch/Covered** — high job value, good margin. Screened porch is a strong secondary.
- **D7 Hyper-local** — cheap, high-intent local ad group.

### BID SELECTIVELY / TEST (secondary budget)
- **F5 Material-specific** — bid vinyl/aluminum (mid-premium, fine). Watch chain link (low-value lean).
- **F6 Gates** — only with qualifiers ("driveway gate", pair with fence); pure "gate repair" is tiny-job risk.
- **F7 HOA/Commercial** — low volume but qualified B2B; test small.
- **D3 Component repair** — only with min-job qualifiers in copy (rail-only jobs are too small alone).
- **D4 Composite/Custom** — evaluate margin fit. iFIXX is **not luxury** — bid composite (mainstream now) but be cautious on "custom/luxury deck" framing.
- **D6 Price-shopping** — bid LOW, route to an estimate/calculator landing, not the main quote page. Localized variants ("deck building cost charlotte") convert better than generic.

### AVOID AT LAUNCH (negative out — see Section 5)
- DIY / how-to ("how to build a deck", "diy fence", "build your own fence").
- Jobs / careers / salary ("fence installer jobs", "deck builder salary", "hiring").
- Cheap / free ("cheap fence", "free fence", "fence for free"). iFIXX is **not "cheapest."**
- Materials-only / retail ("fence panels", "deck boards for sale", "home depot fence", "lowes deck").
- Rent / used ("used fence", "temporary fence rental" — unless a rental line is added later).
- Pure micro-repair with no qualifier where the whole job is a single board/picket.
- Franchise/brand competitor names unless a conquesting test is explicitly funded.

## 5. Recommended Negative-Keyword Seeds

**DIY / how-to / education**
`diy`, `how to`, `how do i`, `build your own`, `build a fence yourself`, `plans`, `tutorial`, `youtube`, `ideas`, `pictures`, `designs` (monitor — some design intent is buyable)

**Jobs / careers / salary**
`job`, `jobs`, `career`, `careers`, `hiring`, `salary`, `pay`, `wage`, `apprentice`, `laborer`, `employment`, `resume`, `subcontractor wanted`

**Cheap / free / discount** (brand rule: not "cheapest")
`cheap`, `cheapest`, `free`, `budget`, `discount`, `coupon`, `grant`, `low cost`, `affordable` (monitor)

**Materials-only / retail / DIY supply**
`home depot`, `lowes`, `lowe's`, `menards`, `costco`, `amazon`, `panels`, `pickets`, `boards for sale`, `kit`, `kits`, `supplies`, `supply`, `material only`, `for sale`, `wholesale`, `bulk`

**Rental / temporary / used**
`rental`, `rent`, `temporary`, `used`, `second hand`, `craigslist`, `facebook marketplace`

**Licensing / regulatory / research non-buyers**
`license`, `licensing`, `permit` (monitor — some buyers search permits), `code`, `hoa rules`, `property line law`, `insurance claim` (monitor — storm-damage buyers)

**Wrong service / off-topic**
`pool` (unless pool-fence line added), `electric fence`, `invisible fence`, `dog fence` (monitor), `wireless dog fence`, `farm fence`, `barbed wire`, `deck of cards`, `pool deck` (unless offered), `boat deck`, `card deck`, `tarot`, `deck stain diy`, `deck cleaning` (unless offered)

**Note:** "insured" not "licensed" — reflect in ad copy, but keep `license`/`licensed` as **monitor-not-hard-block** negatives since some qualified buyers do search "licensed fence contractor."
