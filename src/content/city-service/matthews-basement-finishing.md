## Managing Moisture in Matthews Basements

Charlotte's humid climate means moisture management is critical:

- **Water intrusion assessment** before work begins
- **Exterior drainage** evaluation and recommendations
- **Vapor barriers** on walls and floors
- **Closed-cell spray foam** for insulation that blocks moisture
- **Moisture-resistant drywall** in appropriate areas
- **Dehumidification** sizing and recommendations

We've finished basements throughout Matthews and understand local conditions.
