### What Affects Your Basement Finishing Cost

- **Square footage**: Larger spaces cost more total but often less per square foot
- **Number of rooms**: Dividing space requires more framing and doors
- **Ceiling height**: Low ceilings may require drop ceilings or creative solutions
- **Plumbing additions**: Bathrooms and wet bars significantly increase cost
- **Existing conditions**: Water issues, low ceilings, or HVAC obstacles add complexity
- **Finish level**: Basic vs. premium materials affect final cost
