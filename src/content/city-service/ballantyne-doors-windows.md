## Door and Window Repair in Ballantyne

Ballantyne's beautiful homes are reaching an age where doors and windows need attention. Foundation settling from clay soil, worn hardware after 20+ years, and original windows that don't operate smoothly anymore. We handle it all.

### Common Ballantyne Door & Window Issues

**Settling-Related Door Problems**
Ballantyne's clay soil causes significant settling in homes built during the building boom. Doors that used to close perfectly now rub, stick, or won't latch. We adjust and repair to compensate for settling.

**Worn Window Hardware**
Vinyl windows from the 90s and 2000s have plastic and spring components that wear out. Windows that won't stay open, won't lock properly, or are hard to operate—we can usually repair them.

**Builder-Grade Door Upgrades**
Ready to replace those hollow-core interior doors? We install solid-core and panel door upgrades throughout Ballantyne homes. Pre-hung installation for the best fit and finish.
