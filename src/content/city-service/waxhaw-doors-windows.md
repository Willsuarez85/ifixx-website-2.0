## Quality Door & Window Services in Waxhaw

Well-functioning doors and windows are essential for comfort, security, and energy efficiency. We provide professional door and window installation and repair throughout Waxhaw and Union County.

### Our Waxhaw Door & Window Services

**Entry Doors**: Your front door makes the first impression. We install fiberglass, steel, and wood entry doors in styles from traditional to contemporary. Upgrade to decorative glass, sidelites, and premium hardware for maximum curb appeal.

**Interior Doors**: Bedroom doors, bathroom doors, closet doors, and more. We install pre-hung doors, slab doors, and pocket doors. Upgrade from builder hollow-core to solid wood for better sound insulation.

**Patio & French Doors**: Sliding doors, hinged patio doors, and French doors that connect your indoor and outdoor living spaces. We ensure proper installation for smooth operation and weathertight seals.

**Storm & Screen Doors**: Add protection and ventilation to your entries. We install full-view storm doors, retractable screen doors, and traditional screen doors.

**Window Replacement**: Upgrade to energy-efficient windows that reduce drafts and lower utility bills. We install vinyl, wood, and composite windows in all styles.

**Repairs & Hardware**: Fix sticking doors, replace broken hardware, adjust windows that won't stay open, and solve common door and window problems.
