### Our Waxhaw TV Mounting Services

**Standard Wall Mounting**: Secure mounting on drywall, ensuring the TV is level, stable, and at the right height for comfortable viewing. We locate studs and use appropriate hardware for your TV's weight.

**Concealed Wiring**: No one wants to see cables hanging below their mounted TV. We run wires through the wall and install in-wall power kits for a clean, professional look.

**Fireplace Mounting**: Above-fireplace mounting requires special consideration for heat and viewing angles. We use appropriate mounts and positioning to create a comfortable viewing experience.

**Full-Motion Mounts**: Articulating mounts let you adjust your TV's position—perfect for corner installations or rooms with multiple seating areas.

**Large TV Installation**: Big screens (75"+) require heavy-duty mounts and careful installation. We have experience with the largest consumer TVs and ensure they're properly secured.

### Mount Types Explained

**Fixed Mounts**: Low profile, keeps TV close to wall. Best for dedicated viewing positions where you won't need to adjust angles.

**Tilting Mounts**: Allows vertical angle adjustment. Ideal for above-fireplace installations or high mounting positions.

**Full-Motion Mounts**: Extends from wall and swivels left/right. Perfect for corner installations, large rooms, or when you want flexibility.

**Pull-Down Mounts**: Special mounts for above-fireplace that lower the TV for comfortable viewing. Premium option for fireplace installations.
