## Quality Carpentry Services in Waxhaw

Fine carpentry adds character and value to any home. We provide expert trim work, custom built-ins, and woodworking throughout Waxhaw and Union County with the attention to detail that quality carpentry requires.

### Our Waxhaw Carpentry Services

**Crown Molding**: Crown molding transforms plain rooms into elegant spaces. We install all profiles from simple cove molding to elaborate multi-piece assemblies. Proper installation with tight joints and clean corners is our standard.

**Trim & Baseboards**: Baseboards, door casings, window trim, and chair rails. We install new trim and repair or replace damaged pieces, matching existing profiles throughout your home.

**Custom Built-Ins**: Bookcases, entertainment centers, mudroom cubbies, window seats, and more. We design and build custom pieces that fit your space perfectly and match your home's style.

**Wainscoting & Paneling**: Add architectural detail with wainscoting, board and batten, shiplap, or raised paneling. These treatments transform plain walls into design features.

**Stair Work**: Railings, balusters, newel posts, and treads. We repair, replace, and upgrade stair components to refresh your home's appearance and ensure safety.

**Repair & Restoration**: Damaged trim, rotted wood, and worn woodwork restored or replaced. We match existing profiles to maintain your home's character.

### Why Quality Carpentry Matters

The difference between good carpentry and great carpentry is in the details:

- **Tight joints**: Corners and connections that fit perfectly
- **Level and plumb**: Straight lines throughout
- **Proper wood selection**: Right materials for each application
- **Clean caulking and finishing**: Professional final touches
- **Matching existing work**: New additions that blend seamlessly

These details are what separate handyman work from true craftsmanship.
