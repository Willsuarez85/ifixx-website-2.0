## Matthews Drywall Repair Services

Matthews is one of our favorite areas to work—friendly homeowners and well-maintained homes. We repair drywall throughout Matthews and surrounding communities, from quick doorknob hole patches to major water damage repairs.

### Common Matthews Drywall Problems

**Settling Cracks**
Matthews homes, especially those in newer subdivisions, experience settling that shows up as cracks in drywall. We repair these cracks properly—cutting out the damaged tape, re-taping, and finishing for a lasting repair.

**Water Damage**
Basement moisture and upstairs bathroom leaks cause water damage throughout Matthews. We repair the drywall after the source is fixed, ensuring a clean finish that hides all evidence of the damage.

**Standard Wear and Tear**
Doorknob holes, nail pops, furniture dings—everyday life takes a toll on walls. We fix it all, matching your existing texture so repairs disappear.
