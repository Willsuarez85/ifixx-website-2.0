## Basement Considerations

### Moisture
The #1 concern for basements. We assess water history, check for signs of moisture, and recommend appropriate solutions before finishing.

### Ceiling Height
NC code requires 7' minimum ceiling height in habitable spaces. We work with your existing height or discuss options if it's marginal.

### Egress
Bedrooms require emergency escape—typically an egress window. We help plan code-compliant layouts.
