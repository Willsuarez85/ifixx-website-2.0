### Factors That Affect Your Charlotte Basement Finishing Cost

- **Square footage**: Larger basements cost more but have lower per-square-foot costs
- **Moisture remediation**: If waterproofing is needed before finishing, add $5,000-$15,000
- **Bathroom addition**: A half bath adds $8,000-$12,000; full bath adds $15,000-$25,000
- **Ceiling height**: Low ceilings may limit options; some older Charlotte homes have 7-foot ceilings
- **Egress requirements**: New egress windows for bedrooms add $2,000-$4,000 each
