## Basement Finishing in Rock Hill

Your unfinished basement is wasted space—square footage you own but can't use. iFixx transforms basements throughout Rock Hill and York County into functional living spaces that add value to your home and room to your life.

### What Your Basement Can Become

**Family Room / Living Space**: The most popular basement finishing option. A comfortable space for the family to spread out, watch TV, and hang out.

**Home Theater**: Dark basement spaces are perfect for projectors and big screens. We can build platforms for tiered seating and install acoustic treatments.

**Home Office**: Quiet, separate from the rest of the house, and fully finished. Perfect for work-from-home professionals.

**Guest Suite**: A private space for visitors with bedroom, sitting area, and often a bathroom. Egress window required for bedrooms.

**Game Room / Recreation**: Pool table, bar area, gaming setup—whatever recreation means to you.

**Home Gym**: Rubber flooring, mirrors, proper ventilation, and no more gym membership.

### Our Basement Finishing Process

1. **Assessment**: We evaluate your basement for moisture issues, structural considerations, and code requirements (egress, ceiling height, etc.)

2. **Design & Planning**: We plan the layout, identify electrical and plumbing needs, and create a scope of work. Permits pulled as needed.

3. **Framing**: Walls go up to define rooms and create spaces for mechanicals access.

4. **Rough-In**: Electrical wiring, plumbing (if bathrooms included), and HVAC extensions.

5. **Insulation & Drywall**: Proper insulation for comfort, then drywall, tape, and mud.

6. **Finishing**: Flooring, trim, painting, fixtures, and all the details that make a space livable.

7. **Final Walkthrough**: We ensure everything meets code and your expectations.

### Critical Basement Considerations

**Moisture**: The #1 enemy of finished basements. We assess moisture levels before starting and use appropriate materials (waterproof flooring, moisture-resistant drywall, proper vapor barriers).

**Egress**: If your basement will include a bedroom, code requires an egress window. No exceptions. We can coordinate installation if you don't have one.

**Ceiling Height**: Minimum ceiling height for finished basements is 7 feet in most areas. Low ceilings may limit finishing options or require ceiling treatments.

**Mechanicals Access**: Water heaters, HVAC units, and electrical panels need accessible. We design around these and ensure required clearances.
