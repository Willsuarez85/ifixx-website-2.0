## Managing Moisture in Ballantyne Basements

Charlotte's humid subtropical climate requires attention to moisture:

- **Water intrusion assessment** before any work begins
- **Exterior drainage** evaluation and recommendations
- **Vapor barriers** on walls and floors
- **Closed-cell spray foam** for insulation that blocks moisture
- **Moisture-resistant drywall** and materials
- **Dehumidification** sizing and recommendations
