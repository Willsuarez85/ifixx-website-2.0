## Create Your Perfect Outdoor Living Space in Waxhaw

Waxhaw's mild climate and beautiful surroundings make outdoor living a year-round pleasure. We design and build outdoor spaces that extend your home's living area—from simple deck additions to complete outdoor entertainment areas with kitchens and covered structures.

### Our Waxhaw Outdoor Living Services

**Deck Construction**: Custom decks in pressure-treated wood or low-maintenance composite. We design decks that complement your home and fit your lifestyle, from cozy spaces off the kitchen to expansive multi-level structures.

**Screened Porches**: Enjoy the outdoors without bugs. We build new screened porches or convert existing decks and patios. Add ceiling fans, lighting, and comfortable furnishings for a true outdoor room.

**Covered Patios**: Protection from sun and rain with covered structures. Options include solid roofs, pergolas with shade options, and retractable systems.

**Pergolas & Gazebos**: Add architectural interest and shade to your outdoor space. Free-standing or attached to your home, these structures define outdoor areas beautifully.

**Outdoor Kitchens**: From simple grill stations to complete outdoor kitchens with sinks, refrigerators, and storage. Perfect for Waxhaw's love of outdoor entertaining.

**Deck Repairs & Staining**: Restore your existing deck with repairs, board replacement, and fresh stain or sealant.
