New flooring transforms any room. Whether you want the warmth of hardwood, the durability of tile, or the versatility of luxury vinyl, iFixx delivers expert installation that looks beautiful and lasts for years.

## Flooring Options

### Hardwood
Classic beauty that adds warmth and value. Available in solid and engineered options for different applications.

### Luxury Vinyl Plank (LVP)
Waterproof, durable, and available in realistic wood and stone looks. Perfect for any room, including kitchens and bathrooms.

### Laminate
Budget-friendly with great looks and easy maintenance. Modern laminates are surprisingly durable.

### Tile & Stone
Porcelain, ceramic, natural stone, and large-format tiles for any design aesthetic.

### Carpet
Soft, warm, and available in countless colors and textures for bedrooms and living areas.

## Why Professional Installation Matters

Flooring is a significant investment, and proper installation is crucial for:

- **Longevity** - Correctly installed floors last longer
- **Appearance** - Proper techniques ensure seamless, professional results
- **Warranty** - Many manufacturers require professional installation
- **Time** - We complete projects efficiently with minimal disruption

## Our Installation Process

1. **Measure & Quote** - Accurate measurements and transparent pricing
2. **Material Delivery** - We coordinate material delivery and acclimation
3. **Prep Work** - Remove old flooring, repair subfloors, level as needed
4. **Installation** - Expert installation with proper techniques
5. **Finishing** - Trim, transitions, and thorough cleanup
