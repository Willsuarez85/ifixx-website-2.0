Transform your living space with a professionally mounted TV. No more bulky furniture, tangled cables, or TV at the wrong height. We mount your TV securely and hide the wires for a clean, modern look.

## TV Mounting Services

### Mount Types
- **Fixed Mounts** - Low profile, TV sits flat against wall
- **Tilting Mounts** - Angle TV downward for high mounting or glare reduction
- **Full-Motion Mounts** - Extend, swivel, and tilt for flexible viewing

### Wire Concealment
- **Cable Covers** - Simple, paintable covers that blend with your wall
- **In-Wall Routing** - Wires run inside the wall for completely hidden cables
- **Outlet Relocation** - We move outlets behind the TV for the cleanest look

### Additional Services
- Soundbar mounting
- Streaming device installation
- Smart TV setup
- Multiple TV installations

## Why Professional Installation?

### Safety
TVs are heavy and expensive. Improper mounting can damage your TV, wall, or worse - injure someone. We ensure secure mounting into studs with appropriate hardware.

### Optimal Placement
We consider viewing angles, lighting, and room layout to find the perfect position for your TV.

### Clean Results
No visible wires, level mounting, and professional finish that you'll enjoy every day.

### Time Savings
We handle everything from unpacking to connecting your devices, usually in under two hours.
