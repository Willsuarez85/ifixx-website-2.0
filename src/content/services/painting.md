## The iFIXX Painting Process

We follow a proven process that delivers professional results every time.

### Step 1: Free Color Consultation

**Not sure what colors to choose?** We help. We discuss your vision, consider lighting and room size, recommend color palettes, and provide paint samples if needed.

**We'll be honest:** Some colors look great on a tiny swatch but terrible on four walls. We guide you toward choices that work in your space.

### Step 2: Surface Preparation (The Critical Step)

**This is where most DIY painting fails.** Great paint over bad prep = bad results.

**Our prep process:**
- **Clean surfaces** ‚Äî Remove dust, grease, and grime
- **Fill holes and cracks** ‚Äî Spackle nail holes, fill larger damage
- **Sand smooth** ‚Äî Smooth out patches and glossy surfaces
- **Caulk gaps** ‚Äî Seal gaps between trim and walls
- **Protect everything** ‚Äî Drop cloths on floors, plastic over furniture
- **Tape edges** ‚Äî Clean lines on trim and ceilings
- **Prime when needed** ‚Äî New drywall, stains, color changes, or bare wood

**Why this matters:** Paint doesn't hide imperfections‚Äîit highlights them. Proper prep creates a smooth canvas for flawless results.

### Step 3: Professional Paint Application

**We apply 2-3 coats** (depending on color change and coverage). Each coat is applied with proper technique and allowed to dry completely before the next.

**Interior walls & ceilings:**
- Brush-cut edges and corners
- Roller application with proper nap for your texture
- Even coverage, no roller marks or streaks
- Adequate dry time between coats

**Trim & detail work:**
- Detailed brushwork for crisp lines
- No drips, sags, or visible brush marks
- Smooth, factory-like finish

**What paint we use:**
Primarily Sherwin-Williams and Benjamin Moore‚Äîprofessional-grade paints with better coverage, durability, and color retention than big-box store brands. We can also work with your preferred brand if you have one.

### Step 4: Final Walkthrough & Cleanup

**Before we leave:**
- Walk through every room with you
- Inspect for any missed spots or imperfections
- Touch up as needed
- Remove all tape and protection
- Vacuum and wipe down surfaces
- Haul away all debris

**Your space will be cleaner than when we arrived.** We respect your home.
