## Comprehensive Kitchen Remodeling Services

At iFixx, we handle every aspect of your kitchen transformation. Our experienced team manages the entire process, from initial design to final walkthrough.

### Custom Cabinetry

Cabinets set the tone for your entire kitchen. We offer three tiers to fit any budget:

- **Stock cabinets:** Quality pre-manufactured options in standard sizes—perfect for budget-conscious homeowners
- **Semi-custom cabinets:** More style and size options with upgraded features like soft-close drawers
- **Custom cabinets:** Built to your exact specifications, maximizing every inch of space

Our design team helps you choose the right cabinet style, finish, and hardware to match your vision. From classic raised-panel doors to sleek modern flat-fronts, we've installed them all.

### Countertops That Make a Statement

Your countertops need to be both beautiful and durable. We install all the premium materials Charlotte homeowners love:

- **Granite:** Natural stone with unique patterns—heat resistant and timeless
- **Quartz:** Engineered for consistency and low maintenance—no sealing required
- **Marble:** Elegant veining for a luxurious look—perfect for baking enthusiasts
- **Butcher block:** Warm wood tones that add character
- **Solid surface:** Seamless appearance with easy repairs

We work with trusted local suppliers to ensure you get the best materials at competitive prices.

### Backsplash & Tile Work

A well-designed backsplash protects your walls and adds visual interest. Our tile experts install:

- Subway tile (classic and clean)
- Mosaic patterns (artistic focal points)
- Natural stone (texture and depth)
- Glass tile (modern shimmer)
- Ceramic and porcelain (endless options)

We ensure precise cuts, even grout lines, and proper sealing for long-lasting beauty.

### Flooring Installation

Kitchen floors endure heavy traffic, spills, and temperature changes. We install durable, stylish options:

- **Luxury vinyl plank (LVP):** Waterproof, affordable, and realistic wood look
- **Tile:** Porcelain or ceramic in countless styles
- **Hardwood:** Timeless warmth (with proper moisture management)
- **Laminate:** Budget-friendly with modern looks

Our installation team ensures level subfloors and professional finishing at every transition.

### Lighting & Electrical Upgrades

Good lighting transforms how your kitchen looks and functions. We install:

- Recessed LED lights for even ambient lighting
- Under-cabinet task lighting for prep areas
- Pendant lights over islands and bars
- Dimmer switches for mood control
- Additional outlets for appliances

All electrical work is performed by licensed professionals and inspected to code.

### Plumbing Fixtures & Appliances

We handle all plumbing updates and appliance installations:

- New sinks (undermount, farmhouse, double-bowl)
- Modern faucets with pull-down sprayers
- Dishwasher installation
- Refrigerator water line hookups
- Gas line connections for ranges
- Garbage disposal installation

Your new appliances will be properly connected, level, and ready to use.

### Kitchen Islands

A well-designed island adds workspace, storage, and seating. We custom-build islands to fit your space, whether you need:

- Extra prep space with butcher block tops
- Storage with cabinets and drawers
- Seating for casual dining
- Integrated appliances (wine fridge, microwave drawer)
- Electrical outlets and USB ports

## The iFIXX Kitchen Remodeling Process

We've refined our process through 150+ kitchen projects. Here's what to expect:

**1. Free Consultation & Design (Week 1)**

We meet at your home to discuss your vision, measure your space, and understand your needs. We'll talk about:
- Your cooking and entertaining habits
- Storage needs and pain points
- Style preferences and inspiration
- Budget and timeline

If you'd like, we can create 3D renderings to help you visualize the final result.

**2. Material Selection (Week 1-2)**

We'll guide you through selecting cabinets, countertops, flooring, fixtures, and finishes. Our relationships with suppliers mean you get quality materials at contractor pricing. We provide samples so you can see how everything looks together.

**3. Detailed Estimate & Timeline (Week 2)**

You'll receive a comprehensive written estimate breaking down all costs. We'll also provide a detailed timeline so you know what to expect each week.

**4. Demolition & Preparation (Week 3)**

Once you approve the plan, we start by carefully removing old cabinets, countertops, and fixtures. We protect your floors and other areas of your home. All debris is hauled away daily—no mess left behind.

**5. Rough-In Work (Week 3-4)**

Our licensed electricians and plumbers run new lines as needed. This is when we relocate outlets, install new lighting circuits, and update plumbing for your new layout.

**6. Installation (Week 4-7)**

This is where your new kitchen comes to life:
- Cabinet installation and leveling
- Countertop templating and installation
- Backsplash tile work
- Flooring installation
- Fixture and appliance installation
- Painting and trim work

**7. Finishing Touches (Week 7-8)**

We install hardware, make final adjustments, and ensure every detail is perfect. We clean thoroughly and walk you through your new kitchen, showing you how everything works.

**8. Final Walkthrough**

We don't consider the job complete until you're completely satisfied. We address any concerns and make sure you love your new space.

**Medium Kitchen Remodel: $15,000 - $30,000**
- New semi-custom cabinets
- Premium countertops (quartz/granite)

**Full Kitchen Renovation: $30,000 - $60,000+**
- Custom cabinetry

**Cost factors we consider:**
- Kitchen size (square footage)
- Cabinet quality and customization
- Countertop material
- Appliance selection
- Structural modifications
- Flooring type
- Lighting complexity

We offer free, detailed estimates so you know exactly what to expect. We can also work with you to prioritize upgrades that fit your budget while delivering maximum impact.
